# Outline Quality Rubric

Before finalizing an outline, check:

- Coverage: all core arguments and innovation points appear in coverage matrices.
- Evidence: major body sections list relevant supporting papers when the literature report provides them.
- Logic: `logic_graph` explains how sections connect.
- Word budget: positive budgets sum to the requested total.
- Paper type: survey papers do not contain unsupported experiment/result sections.
- Weak support: weakly supported claims are framed as gaps, discussion, limitations, or future trends.
- Downstream usability: every body section has core points, evidence notes, and transition notes.
