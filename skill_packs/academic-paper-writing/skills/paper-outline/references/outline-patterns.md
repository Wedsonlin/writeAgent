# Outline Patterns

## Survey Paper

Use this when `paper_type` is `survey`, `review`, `综述`, or when the task emphasizes field context and literature synthesis.

Recommended structure:

1. 摘要
2. 引言
3. 研究现状与领域脉络
4. 主题/技术/系统分类比较
5. 核心论点综合与创新点展开
6. 研究缺口、挑战与未来趋势
7. 结论
8. 参考文献

Do not add “实验” or “结果” sections unless the task explicitly asks for empirical evaluation.

## Empirical Paper

Use this when the task includes dataset, method, experiment, result, ablation, or evaluation requirements.

Recommended structure: 摘要、引言、相关工作、方法、实验设计、结果、讨论、结论、参考文献。

## Theoretical Paper

Use this when the task asks for a conceptual model, theory, proposition, or framework without experiments.

Recommended structure: 摘要、引言、相关研究、理论框架、命题/模型、分析与讨论、结论、参考文献。
