# Word Budget Rubric

When `writing_task.word_limit.by_chapter` is null, allocate from `writing_task.word_limit.total`.

## Survey Default

For a survey paper:

- 摘要: about 5% to 7%
- 引言: about 12% to 15%
- 研究现状与领域脉络: about 18% to 22%
- 主题/技术/系统比较: about 25% to 32%
- 研究缺口、挑战与趋势: about 15% to 20%
- 结论: about 6% to 8%
- 参考文献: 0 words

The final plan must sum to the requested total for all positive-budget sections.

## Principle

Budget should follow argumentative importance and evidence density. Avoid equal splitting unless the upstream task explicitly provides equal chapter budgets.
