# Argument Section Mapping

Map every core argument and innovation point into the outline.

## Required Mapping

- Each `writing_task.core_arguments[]` item must appear in `outline.argument_coverage[]`.
- Each `writing_task.innovation_points[]` item must appear in `outline.innovation_coverage[]`.
- Coverage entries must list section ids, supporting papers, support strength, and treatment.
- Major body sections should include at least one linked argument or linked innovation unless they are 摘要、参考文献, or purely transitional.

## Weak Support Policy

If support strength is `weak`, do not present the item as a firm conclusion. Use one of these treatments:

- 研究缺口
- 讨论
- 限制
- 未来趋势

Only `strong` or `moderate` support may be framed as an established conclusion.
