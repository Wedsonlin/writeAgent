# Argument Consistency

Before finalizing the draft, check that each substantive section serves the paper's argument rather than merely filling space.

Required checks:

- Every major core argument appears in `argument_trace`.
- Every major innovation point appears in `innovation_trace`.
- Each section has `linked_core_arguments` or a clear structural role such as abstract, conclusion, or references.
- Weak literature support cannot become a strong conclusion.
- Terms used for the same concept remain consistent across sections.
- `transition_in` and `transition_out` explain how sections connect.

If a section introduces a claim that cannot be traced to literature evidence, research data, or explicit user claims, add it to `unsupported_claims` and rewrite the body as a limitation, research gap, or open question.
