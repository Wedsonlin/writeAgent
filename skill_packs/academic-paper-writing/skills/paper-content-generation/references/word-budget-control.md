# Word Budget Control

Use Skill3's word budget as the drafting target.

Rules:

- `target_word_count` comes from `outline.sections[].word_budget` when available.
- A section may vary by roughly 25% when evidence density requires it.
- If a section is much shorter than its target, expand with evidence-backed comparison, limitation analysis, or transition logic.
- If a section is much longer than its target, compress background and keep argument-bearing paragraphs.
- Record large deviations in `quality_checks.word_budget_warnings`.

Never pad a section with repeated templates or generic explanation.
