# Chinese Academic Prose

Use formal Chinese academic prose by default.

Preferred style:

- Write coherent paragraphs with clear topic sentences and evidence-backed analysis.
- Use precise verbs: "表明", "揭示", "说明", "支撑", "限制", "提示".
- Distinguish fact, interpretation, comparison, limitation, and future work.
- Keep English only for native terms, titles, names, systems, models, citation keys, URLs, and DOI values.

Avoid:

- Oral phrases such as "我们可以看到", "很厉害", "大家都知道".
- Template openings repeated across sections.
- Bullet-only content for substantive sections.
- Empty summaries such as "本节围绕相关问题展开讨论" without evidence or argument.
- Claims about performance, novelty, trend, or consensus without citation or data.
